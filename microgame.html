MICROGAME DESIGNS

I create interactive microgames that engage users and match the client’s brand. Each game is designed to be unique and provide a memorable experience. These games help boost user interaction and brand awareness.

OKC THUNDER PLINKO

I designed an interactive Plinko game for the OKC Thunder, one of several games I've created for them from the 2023-2024 season to the 2024-2025 season, engaging fans with chances to win exclusive items and Thunder Shop discounts.

LIQUID DEATH SCRATCH OFF

I created a scratch-off mockup for Liquid Death, featuring an interactive chance to win VIP concert passes. The design combines the excitement of a hidden prize with Liquid Death’s bold, edgy brand style, offering fans an engaging and memorable experience.

TEXAS ROADHOUSE SPIN TO WIN

This is one of the several mockups I designed for Texas Roadhouse, featuring a spin-to-win game that offers users a chance to win a prize.
